# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Throughstone to be a welcoming, friendly, and harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. Everyone who participates — in issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other project space — is expected to help keep it that way.

## Our standards

We ask everyone in the community to:

- Be respectful, kind, and considerate in all interactions.
- Welcome newcomers and assume good intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

Behavior that makes the community unwelcoming or unsafe for others is not acceptable. This
includes harassment of any kind. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and
participation that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Our standard in full

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text — including the
detailed standards, enforcement guidelines, and definitions — is the authoritative reference
for this Code of Conduct and is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any concerns, please report it
privately to the maintainer at **hershey@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and
handled with discretion. The maintainer is responsible for enforcement and will respect the
privacy and safety of anyone who reports an issue.
